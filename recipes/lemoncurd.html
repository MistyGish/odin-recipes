Lemon Curd

Beautifully tart lemon curd-- the perfect match for scones!

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cubed unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp grated lemon zest

Steps:

  1. Combine ingredients in saucepan.
  2. Cook on medium to low heat, whisking constantly until it thickens and bubbles.
