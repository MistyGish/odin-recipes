Lemon Cake

A perfectly moist lemon cake to share on all occasions!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and grease 9in. square pan.
  2. Beat together sugar and butter, then beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Sift together flour and baking powder and mix into cream. Then add milk, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  4. Pour batter in pan and bake until toothpick can be inserted and removed, clean.
